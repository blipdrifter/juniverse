Links

  • Nifflas' Site
    The website for games made by Nicklas "Nifflas" Nygren, such as Within a Deep Forest or the Knytt series.
  • Nifflas' Support Forum
    The place where you can still find the somewhat-active members of the Nifflas community. A lot of new KS levels still get posted here!
  • Knytt Stories Level Archive
    The ultimate collection of Knytt Stories levels. You can find almost every level ever made here!
  • Knytt Stories Plus
    A mod for Knytt Stories made by Egomassive that allows for much more complex level features, such as captions, signs with multiple screens, etc. (You also need it installed for any levels created using Knytt Stories Plus.)
