Welcome

...to the Juniverse. This site covers various games in the Knytt and Within a Deep Forest series, created by Nifflas. It also features a hand-picked selection of Knytt Stories levels and level editing resources.

What is Knytt?

The Knytt series was created by Nicklas 'Nifflas' Nygren, and contains many of his games, such as the original Knytt, which tells the story of a lost knytt searching for ship parts, Knytt Stories, a game based on the original with a new level editor and a large following, Knytt Experiment, and the newest, Knytt Underground. Within a Deep Forest also takes place in the same universe.

Knytt and Knytt Stories can be downloaded for free from nifflas.com.

Knytt Undeground is available on Steam or through Fastspring for $7.99.

This is a fan-made website.

The Juniverse is a fansite and is not associated with Nifflas in any way.
