Knytt Stories Advanced is one of the first mods to Knytt Stories that allows Knytt Stories to have a few more features than the vanilla game. However, KS Advanced levels have to be played with KS Advanced, and not just vanilla KS or any other mod. This is different from later mods because it actually has no modified level editor included with it.


Some of the features included in the mod include:

  • jump-through platforms
  • blood that acts like rain
  • wall-mounted turrets and spotlights
  • a new, robotic-like "seeker" enemy
  • a robotic cannon
  • a timer object
  • dark "cloud" puffs
  • and more.


Knytt Stories Advanced is one of the first known mods for Knytt Stories, and therefore is much more basic compared to mods that came out later on. However, development has ceased for the mod in late 2009, and is no longer being updated. It isn't recommended you use this mod, however it is nice to look back and see what the KS modding scene used to be like.
