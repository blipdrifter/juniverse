Resources

This part of the Juniverse is still under construction. Currently, this is where we'll post free-to-use tilesets and custom objects, whether they're made by Noah or Marin or links to forum posts with more content.

READ BEFORE CLICKING ANYTHING:

We highly recommend you don't use copyrighted content in your level (such as music, sounds, or graphics). Make sure everything you use uses a free license (such as one of the Creative Commons licenses) or you have permission from the owner of the content you're using. See here for more info.

You can click on a license next to any link and it'll show you the license page.
